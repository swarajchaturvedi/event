Aim:

To promote AATMA NIRBHARTA among children by encouraging them to build innovative toys based on the Indian value system which will show positive behavior and good value.

About

About

Under the aegis of GLA University, Mathura #AatmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan we collect new innovative ideas about the toy and game industry from young minds based on Indian values and culture. ToyTrump is a unique opportunity for Students in India to submit their innovative toys/games concepts and get funds upto Rs. 50 lakhs. Winning Teams efforts will commercialize the exceptional toy concepts and will get support from Industry and investors, with investment upto 2.5 lakh* - 50 lakh**.

Objective:-

The goal of the “toytrump” is to promote India as a global toy manufacturing hub. This toytrump will enable students from schools to build, as design experts, toy experts and startups, and get together and create ideas for developing toys and games that are based on Indian culture and Indian value systems.

Eligibility criteria

In order to be eligible for the competition it is very necessary to meet all the rules and regulations of the eligibility criteria. At the commencement of the competition it is necessary that the student participating is strictly a student of class 11th, 12th or 12 +1 (dropping one year). Anyone else participating in the competition will be deemed ineligible for the competition.

Problem statement

As the Govt. and the people of India are boycotting the use of the Chinese products including the mass-produced toys and games, this gives an enormous opportunity to cover the need of toys and games, by producing them locally as well as reviving native games and toys.

Evaluation

The evaluation will be done by the honourable judges as well as representatives from the GLA University. The decision taken by judges will be via voting and thus the result will be reached.
The followings are the parameters of evaluation-

  • 1.Feasibility of the idea
  • 2.Succes Potential of the idea
  • 3.Impact/Potential/sustainability in the Society
  • 4.Market Size and Growth
