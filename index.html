The mission of this campaign is to promote the vision of our Honourable Prime Minister “Atma Nirbhar Indian Toys” amongst the youth of India.  

About

About

Toy Trump is a national level competition to encourage Indian Youth for transforming their innovations into reality concerning with Indian Toys. Recently our Honourable Prime Minister highlighted the great potential of Indian Toys, citing the nation’s rich tradition, culture, heritage and characters. 
Under the aegis of GLA University, Mathura #Atma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan, innovative ideas from Indian young minds have been called in collaboration with Innovation2reality. Toy Trump is providing an opportunity to get a fund up-to 50 lakhs* for such innovative toys/games’ concepts. The aforementioned funds will be provided to Winning Teams/individuals for transforming their virgin ideas into start-ups.
ToyTrump 2021 is a unique opportunity for students in India to submit their innovative toys/games concepts and get funds uptoRs. 50 lakhs.

Objective:-

To nurture and execute the novel innovative toys/games ideas primarily based on Indian value system which eventually reflects a positive behaviour amongst the Indian youth.

Eligibility criteria

People from age group 14-20 years can participate in the Toy Trump 2021. Anyone else participating in the competition will be deemed ineligible for the competition.

Problem statement

As the Govt. and the people of India are boycotting the use of Chinese products including mass-produced toys and games, this gives an enormous opportunity to cover the need for toys and games, by producing them locally as well as reviving native games and toys.

Evaluation

The evaluation will be done by the honorable judges from corporate and Industry experts. 
The followings are the parameters of evaluation-

  1. Feasibility of the idea
  2. Success Potential of the idea
  3. Impact/Potential/sustainability in the Society
  4. Market Size and Growth

Launch of toyTrump:

The launch of ToyTrump 2021 has the motto to create an ecosystem for the domestic Toy industry and the local market. ToyTrump 2021 is a unique opportunity for students across India to submit their innovative toys/games concepts and win huge prizes upto worth Rs. 50 lakhs.

Registration and Idea Submission:

If you have any idea for a toy/game, you should prepare the remaining requirements in the form and submit the idea in the prescribed format. The last date of submission of ideas is 7th August, 2021.

  • Stage 1 – Online Evaluation:

    An online evaluation by a team of expert evaluators will examine the ideas submitted.The evaluators will judge your idea based on predetermined criteria.
    Evaluation of ideas starts from 7th August to 13th August.

  • Stage 2 - Final ceremony:

    This ceremony will take place through an online platform (date to be announced). All the top 10 teams/individuals are invited here to discuss over their ideas and to promote them on industry level.

    • Declaration of Results:

    Based on the evaluation, the best ideas selected will receive prizes. Commencement of results will be on 14th -15th August, 2021. Respective teams/individuals will be notified via mail or message. 

    METHODOLOGY:

    The ToyTrump is based on nine themes — Indian culture, history, knowledge of India and ethos; learning, education and schooling; social and human values; occupations and specific fields; environment; divyang; fitness and sport; out-of-the-box, and rediscovering traditional Indian toys.

    This is high time, we want to utilize the brightest and most creative minds to carve out games based on our Indian culture, tradition, and heritage and stories of ancient India showcasing the beliefs and traditions of people.

    Instructions:-

    Provide information about your innovative idea, based on the following questions in not more than 300 words each. You are free to submit your idea in a simple description, sketch of your idea, or by Powerpoint presentation.

    Requirements :-

    1. Overview of the idea.
      विचार का अवलोकन.
      Briefly share your idea and concept. Minute details are not needed.
      अपने विचार और अवधारणा को संक्षेप में साझा करें। विवरण की आवश्यकता नहीं है।
    2. Approach towards the development of the idea during the TOYTRUMP
      संक्षेप में विचार के विकास की दिशा में आपका दृष्टिकोण.
      Share a basic development flow.
      एक बुनियादी विकास प्रवाह साझा करें।
    3. How is your idea different from the existing toys / games?
      आपका विचार मौजूदा खिलौनों/खेलों से किस प्रकार भिन्न है?
      Overview of your research on existing toys along with 2 or 3 references (links), and how they differ from your idea.
      2 या 3 संदर्भों (लिंक) के साथ मौजूदा खिलौनों पर आपके शोध का अवलोकन, और वे आपके विचार से कैसे भिन्न हैं।

    General terms and conditions:

  • The idea should be original.
  • This amount will be granted by New-Gen IEDC to students who will become the part of GLA University, Mathura.
  • If the idea is appreciated by New-Gen IEDC, GLA University then the team gets a grant of upto 50 lakhs.
  • If the idea is found to be plagiarized, the student or team will be disqualified from the competition and will no longer be allowed to participate.
  • If any team or student is found to be ineligible according to the eligibility criteria, they will be terminated from participating in the competition.
  • The decision taken by the judges will be final.
